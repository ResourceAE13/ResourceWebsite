Electrical Safety Solution

Electricity is used in almost everything in the workplace. Electrical equipment can cause serious shock and burn injuries if improperly used. Some of the reasons for potential hazard are caused by equipment defect, defect of Tools, lack of distance to live parts, lack of insulating blankets and errors by switching off equipment
If a part of the body comes into contact with the electrical circuit, a shock will occur. The current enters the body at one point and leaves at another point. This passage of electricity can cause great pain, burns, and even fatalities.
