Head Protection

Helmets and Bump caps protect the wearer’s head from impact and penetration by falling or flying objects and from high-voltage shock and burn.
In a time of rapid technological development, we have with us a very powerful portable computer all the time – Our Brain. Unfortunately, our brain is fragile and its packaging is not enough to protect it from all shocks or impacts. So, we need to choose appropriate protection for our brain and head. Such injuries can also lead to neck injuries and eventually spinal injury.
Head injury is still one of the most common causes of fatal injury in accidents. Choosing the right head protection, and then using it correctly, is not that difficult and will protect both your health and your wallet.
  • Type of Head protection should only be used after a risk assessment has been made.
  • Head protection should be selected, used and maintained correctly.
